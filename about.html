About Us

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Evergreen Tea House began as a small passion project by tea enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves access to premium, thoughtfully sourced teas. What started as weekend tea tastings in a cozy backyard has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

Our mission is simple: to share the beauty and benefits of tea culture with our local community. We carefully select each tea from trusted growers around the world, ensuring that every cup tells a story of quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship.
